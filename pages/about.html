About Arees

Arees Advanced Co. established in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia, in 2022. Arees is

the distributor of several high-quality brands in chocolates, confectionary,

biscuits and a wide range of International FMCG Brands.

Vision

We at Arees aspire to become the market leader in the FMCG business by adopting innovative procurement and distribution technologies and enabling access to high quality FMCG products.

Mission

Our mission at Arees is to contribute to the advancement of well-being so that everyone has access to the highest quality basic food and beverages available in the world.

Core Values

We are committed to the responsibility of conducting our business in the most ethical manner along with aligning with the highest quality standards in the industry. Our highly qualified employees perform their duties in a performance-driven structured environment and take responsibility in their actions to create an unparalleled experience for both customers and suppliers.
